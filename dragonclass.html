Dragon Classes

Here you will find information about different dragon classes.
There are 4 main types

  • WarriorStrong and resilient, these dragons excel in physical combat
  • Hunter Agile and stealthy, these dragons are expert trackers and ambushers, able to take down towers at a distance
  • Sorcerer Masters of magic, these dragons wield powerful spells to control the battlefield and support their allies
  • Invoker Unique to War Dragons, Invoker dragons use a special attack cycle: three regular shots followed by a powerful invoker shot, requiring strategic timing and skillful play.

Elements

There are 5 different elements that dragons can possess:

  • Fire - Masters of destruction, fire dragons unleash devastating flames upon their foes.
  • Ice - Cold and calculating, ice dragons freeze their enemies in place, rendering them helpless.
  • Wind - Quick and unpredictable, wind dragons strike with incredible speed and power.
  • Earth - Sturdy and resilient, earth dragons use their strength to protect their allies and control the battlefield.
  • Dark - Mysterious and elusive, dark dragons manipulate shadows to confuse and terrify their enemies.
